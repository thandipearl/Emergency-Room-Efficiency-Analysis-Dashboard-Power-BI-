# Emergency Room Efficiency Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Emergency Room (ER) operations and patient care at a high-volume healthcare facility. Using a dataset of 9,000+ patients (April 2023 – October 2024), the goal was to move from guesswork to data-driven decision-making.

The focus: identify bottlenecks, understand patient demographics and improve how quickly patients receive care.

---

## The Challenge
ER environments are fast-paced and high-stakes. Stakeholders needed visibility into:

**Patient Flow** – Identify peak days and hours  
**Efficiency** – Measure patient wait times  
**Satisfaction** – Understand impact of delays on experience  
**Resource Allocation** – Track referrals across departments  

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Total Patients** – Monitor daily and seasonal trends  
**Average Wait Time** – Time from arrival to first medical attention (35.3 mins)  
**Patient Satisfaction** – Rated on a 1–10 scale (avg: 4.99)  
**Referral Rate** – Distribution across departments (e.g., General Practice, Orthopedics)  

---

## Dashboard Structure

The Power BI report is divided into four main views:

**Monthly View** – Tracks trends in volume and demographics  
**Consolidated View** – High-level performance with dynamic filters  
**Patient Details** – Row-level data for deep analysis  
**Key Takeaways** – Summary of insights and recommendations  

---

## Key Insights & Recommendations

**Staffing Optimization**  
  - Busiest day: Saturday (1,377 patients)  
  - Peak hours: 11 AM, 1 PM, 7 PM, 11 PM  
  → Recommendation: Increase staffing during peak periods  

  **Low Patient Satisfaction**  
  - Average score: 4.99/10  
  → Opportunity to improve experience, especially during peak times  

  **Referral Trends**  
  - General Practice: 1,840  
  - Orthopedics: 995  
  → Strengthen coordination with high-demand departments  

- **Admission Patterns**  
  - ~50% admitted vs discharged  
  → Indicates high case severity

## Dataset

The dataset contains patient-level records, including:

**Demographics** – Age groups, gender, race  
**Timestamps** – Admission dates, wait times  
**Clinical Data** – Referrals, admission status, case managers  

---

## Outcome
This dashboard provides actionable insights to improve ER efficiency, reduce wait times and enhance patient satisfaction through better staffing and resource allocation.
